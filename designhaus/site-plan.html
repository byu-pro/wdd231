DesignHaus Site Plan

Course: WDD 231 | Individual Website Project

Site Name

DesignHaus was selected to represent a refined and modern creative studio. It communicates the professional identity of a brand agency and embodies the aesthetic sensibilities of a designer-led portfolio.

Site Purpose

The purpose of DesignHaus is to showcase my graphic and web design projects in a visually engaging and professional manner. The site will serve as my personal brand platform, allowing potential clients, collaborators, or employers to explore case studies, view work samples, and contact me directly.

Scenarios

  • Where can I explore your past branding or design projects?
  • How do I get in touch with you for freelance or agency work?

Site Map

The website will have the following main pages:

  • Home – Overview of DesignHaus and featured work.
  • Work – Case studies and design portfolio.
  • About – Background, experience, and brand philosophy.
  • Contact – Direct contact form and social media links.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1A1A1D (Dark Black) – used for backgrounds and strong contrast.
  • Accent Color: #6A0DAD (Royal Purple) – used for buttons, highlights, and key UI elements.
  • Secondary Color: #D3D3D3 (Light Grey) – used for text and subtle backgrounds.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat – clean, modern sans-serif for titles.
  • Body Font: Inter – highly readable sans-serif for content.
