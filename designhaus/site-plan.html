DesignHaus Site Plan

Course: WDD 231 | Individual Website Project

Site Name

DesignHaus was selected to represent a refined and modern creative studio. It communicates the professional identity of a brand agency and embodies the aesthetic sensibilities of a designer-led portfolio.

Site Purpose

The purpose of DesignHaus is to showcase my graphic and web design projects in a visually engaging and professional manner. The site will serve as my personal brand platform, allowing potential clients, collaborators, or employers to explore case studies, view work samples, and contact me directly.

Scenarios

  • Where can I explore your past branding or design projects?
  • How do I get in touch with you for freelance or agency work?

Site Map

  • Work: Showcasing my design portfolio and case studies.
  • About: Information about me, my background, and design philosophy.
  • Contact: A form and contact details for potential clients or collaborations.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1A1A1A (Dark Grey/Almost Black) – used for headings and main UI elements.
  • Accent Color: #7D3C98 (Rich Purple) – used for buttons, hover effects, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #4A235A (Deep Purple) – used for section dividers and contrast elements.
  • Background Color: #F0F0F0 (Light Grey) – used for the overall page background.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins – modern sans-serif for section titles and nav elements.
  • Body Font: Inter – clean and readable for paragraphs and content blocks.
  • UI & Buttons Font: Montserrat – stylish sans-serif for buttons and navigation.
